25 !* *
26 !* This file contains the subroutines that determine the diapycnal *
27 !* diffusivity driven by resolved shears, as specified by the *
28 !* parameterizations described in Jackson and Hallberg (JPO, 2008). *
29 !* *
30 !* The technique by which the 6 equations (for kappa, TKE, u, v, T, *
31 !* and S) are solved simultaneously has been dramatically revised *
32 !* from the previous version. The previous version was not converging *
33 !* in some cases, especially near the surface mixed layer, while the *
34 !* revised version does. The revised version solves for kappa and *
35 !* TKE with shear and stratification fixed, then marches the density *
36 !* and velocities forward with an adaptive (and aggressive) time step *
37 !* in a predictor-corrector-corrector emulation of a trapezoidal *
38 !* scheme. Run-time-settable parameters determine the tolerence to *
39 !* which the kappa and TKE equations are solved and the minimum time *
40 !* step that can be taken. *
41 !* *
