25 !* *
26 !* This file contains the subroutine (write_energy) that writes *
27 !* horizontally integrated quantities, such as energies and layer *
28 !* volumes, and other summary information to an output file. Some *
29 !* of these quantities (APE or resting interface height) are defined *
30 !* relative to the global histogram of topography. The subroutine *
31 !* that compiles that histogram (depth_list_setup) is also included *
32 !* in this file. *
33 !* *
34 !* In addition, if the number of velocity truncations since the *
35 !* previous call to write_energy exceeds maxtrunc or the total energy *
36 !* exceeds a very large threshold, the day is increased to Huge_time *
37 !* so that the model will gracefully halt itself. *
38 !* *
39 !* This file also contains a few miscelaneous initialization *
40 !* calls to FMS-related modules. *
41 !* *
42 !* Macros written all in capital letters are defined in MOM_memory.h. *
43 !* *
44 !* A small fragment of the grid is shown below: *
45 !* *
46 !* j+1 x ^ x ^ x At x: q *
47 !* j+1 > o > o > At ^: v *
48 !* j x ^ x ^ x At >: u *
49 !* j > o > o > At o: h, bathyT *
50 !* j-1 x ^ x ^ x *
51 !* i-1 i i+1 At x & ^: *
52 !* i i+1 At > & o: *
53 !* *
54 !* The boundaries always run through q grid points (x). *
55 !* *
